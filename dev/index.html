Data Visualisations

This section contains a collection of data visualisations that I have created using d3.js.

Player comparison

This tool allows you to compare player's chance maps across a variety of leagues. The size of the circle highlights the quality of the chance, whilst the colour showcases the outcome. Hovering over a particular chance will display additional information. The data was provided by StrataData

Premier League week-by-week position line graph

This line graph shows the gameweek-by-gameweek league position of each team in the Premier League 2017/18 season. You can select/unselect certain teams for comparison purposes as well as selecting/unselecting all.

Gambling in Great Britain

Interactive map to show the results of the Gambling Commissions' health report on gambling. You can click on a region to see the results for that particular area (in grey), whilst the average will show in white.

UK Parliamentary Consituencys Map

Map to show the Parliamentary Consituencys in the UK. Click on a region to find out which individual party and individual currently holds that Constituency.
